Hello! Thank you for your interest in me. My name is Thamazgha, and I'm currently twenty six years old. I can't remember when I first started making art, but I know for sure that it has always been part of my life. Throughout the years, I've gone through phases in which I've written stories, learned musical instruments, weaved my own clothes by hand and beaded my own accessories. I've worked with paper, fiber, wood, and all sorts of media through which I could express myself. Needless to say, pen to paper (or pen to tablet, in most showcased art here) remain one of my favorite ways to liberate my artistic visions.
