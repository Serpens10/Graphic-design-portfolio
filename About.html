Hello! Thank you for your interest in me.

My name is Thamazgha, and I'm currently twenty six years old. I can't remember when I first started making art, but I know for sure that it has always been part of my life. Throughout the years, I've gone through phases in which I've written stories, learned musical instruments, weaved my own clothes by hand and beaded my own accessories. I've worked with paper, fiber, wood, and all sorts of media through which I could express myself. Needless to say, pen to paper (or pen to tablet, in most showcased art here) remain one of my favorite ways to liberate my artistic visions.

After I graduated high school, I joined an oil and gas engineering course at the university of Boumerdes. Nowadays, I can't imagine my life without the mechanical engineering knowledge that I gained throughout my 5 year academic journey, but I'm also aware that an engineer is not all who I am, and that I will only truly find happiness when I combine between my two greatest passions in life: Art and Engineering. Following that realization, I decided to enroll in the regional school of fine arts of Oran, and I managed to finish a whole year of the curriculum before personal circumstances pushed me to drop out and move back to my hometown.
